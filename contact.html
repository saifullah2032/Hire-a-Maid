Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hire a maid? +
You can hire a maid by browsing our available professionals, checking their profiles, and booking an appointment online.
Are the maids verified? +
Yes! All maids go through a background check and verification process before they are listed on our platform.
What services do the maids offer? +
Our maids provide cleaning, cooking, babysitting, elderly care, and laundry services. You can choose based on your needs.
What are the charges for hiring a maid? +
The charges vary based on experience, hours of service, and type of work. You can find detailed pricing on each maid's profile.
Can I book a maid for a single day? +
Yes, you can hire a maid for a single day, weekly, or on a long-term contract depending on your requirements.
